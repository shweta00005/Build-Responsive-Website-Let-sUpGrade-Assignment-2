Digital Branding

What is Digital Branding

Digital branding means to create and design your brand’s presence online. The various digital platforms through which this can be achieved are websites, apps, social media, video advertising etc. The scope for digital branding is vast just like the internet itself.

In simple terms, Digital branding = Digital marketing + Internet branding

It can also be described as a communication between a company and its customers.

Importance of Digital Branding

Online presence of your brand is a pre requisite these days, to make an impact on the consumer’s mind. Brands without digital presence are like planes without fuel. Digital branding makes use of the same platforms, people use, to connect with their friends, families, acquaintances. This is how a direct connection is established with your target customers, making them feel & be loyal to your brand. You can also build a database of customer purchase patterns on the basis of the gathered information to further increase your sales.


Channels forming an integral part of Digital Branding are:

1. Search engine optimization

2. Affiliate marketing

3. Social media marketing

4. Content marketing

5. Email marketing

6. Video marketing

7. Website designing

8. Influencer marketing

9. Pay per click

Digital marketing vs Digital branding

Both are different terms. Digital Branding is creating connections between customers and the products/services being delivered, in order to achieve brand awareness, customer loyalty. Driving sales is not necessarily the objective here. Your brand thrives on your brand identity, including your website ,logo, colors, packaging, copy, social media etc. It’s a long term marketing strategy.

Whereas,Digital Marketing is more about driving sales online. It is used to promote your product/ services on digital channels. It is all about finding new customers and generating sales.
